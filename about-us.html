About Us




What started out as a small cafe in chinchwad Pune, today, has become the definition of a delectable taste for food and beverages. A mixture of passion, creativity and determination is what it took to turn Cafe delight into a success story.

But like most successful businesses, success did not happen overnight. The journey of Mr. Samuel.F.Paul, founder of Cafe delight, The long journey started in 2005, he worked for a well-known coffee brand to get expertise in Coffee. Later on, while working with his friend who ran a chain of eateries and served bakery products, he learned all about Bakery products. This powerful combination led to the creation of Cafe delight. However, like most journeys, there are always hurdles to cross. One such hurdle was the Equipment used to make the Coffee. Because of its manual use in cafes, it became quite a task to increase the Sale capacity and keep up with the rising demand. So Mr. Samuel.F.Paul, trained staff on imported machines that could serve more coffee sandwiches & desserts in a short time. So what is so unique about the brand that people keep coming back for more every time? For starters, Cafe Buddy's Espresso is deeply rooted in tradition and uses traditional methods to make coffee, sandwiches & desserts. All the ingredients used are almost completely natural and are sourced from India & out of India. They claim to not use any preservatives, stabilizers and artificial colors or chemicals, which is why their eatables are delicious and of wholesome quality & recipes. With 15 years of being in the business, Cafe Buddy's Espresso is still Raring to go Places. And it is a rare combination of traditional methods and modern technology that, they believe, will take them there.
